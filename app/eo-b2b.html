How does
Standing Orders work?

  • 1. Select your products or easily copy
    one of your past orders
  • 2. Define frequency, starting date,
    delivery and payment methods
  • 3. Receive your order at the frequency
    selected. Enjoy!

Start by selecting your products

Select one of your past orders in your order history (upon products availability) and click on "Make this order recurrent". You can still add or delete products, as well as modify desired quantities at this step.

Or, within your account, access the menu "My Standing Orders" to create a new Standing Order from scratch. You will be able to select all of your Nespresso Grands Crus, and a limited range of accessories and sweets.

Now define the frequency at which you would like to receive your orders

At this step, you will select a pre-defined frequency and the starting date of your Standing Order. To help you, Nespresso can suggest a rcommended frequency based on the contents of your order.

Then define your delivery address along with the delivery and payment methods

You can setup your regular delivery address and payment details.

Easy to change according to your needs

Your frequency as well as your next shipment date, delivery address or payment methods can be modified at any time up to the day before shipment. For instance, during your holidays, you could ship your Standing Order to your vacation address (if within the same country) or simply postpone it.

Receive your order with no extra effort

Based on your defined frequency, you will receive automatically your order at your desired address.

If you find yourself running out of coffee or with too much stock at home, you can easily adjust your next shipment date. If needed, you can create more than one Standing Order and simply manage them individually.
